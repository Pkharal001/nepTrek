Everest Base Camp Trek

Duration: 14 Days | Difficulty: Moderate | From $1299

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu
  • Day 2: Fly to Lukla, Trek to Phakding
  • Day 3: Trek to Namche Bazaar
  • Day 4: Acclimatization Day
  • Day 5-8: Trek through Tengboche, Dingboche, Lobuche
  • Day 9: Reach Everest Base Camp
  • Day 10-13: Return to Lukla
  • Day 14: Fly back to Kathmandu

What's Included

  • Accommodation during the trek
  • Domestic flights (Kathmandu-Lukla-Kathmandu)
  • Licensed English-speaking guide
  • All permits and paperwork

What's Not Included

  • International airfare
  • Personal gear and insurance
  • Tips for guides and porters

Important Info

Max Altitude: 5,364m
Best Season: March-May, Sept-Nov
Group Size: 2-12 people

FAQs

Do I need prior trekking experience?
Not necessary, but good physical fitness is recommended.

What kind of food will be available?
We provide local and international meals (vegetarian available).

Is altitude sickness common?
We take necessary acclimatization days and our guides monitor health closely.
