Our Story

Trekking with Heart, Spirit & Adventure

Who We Are

NepalTrek was founded by a team of passionate adventurers and native guides who believe trekking is more than just reaching a destination—it's about experiencing Nepal's rich culture, diverse landscapes, and the warmth of its people. After years of guiding international travelers across Himalayan trails, we realized the need for a company that offers authentic, safe, and personalized trekking experiences. Thus, NepalTrek was born.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide unforgettable trekking experiences that connect people with nature and culture, while ensuring sustainability, safety, and respect for local communities.
